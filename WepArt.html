Inspired by the timeless elegance of charcoal and attention to detail..


Charcoal drawing is one of the oldest forms of art and is considered a traditional medium used by artists since ancient times, such as in the cave paintings of Lascaux. As a drawing tool, charcoal is distinguished by its ability to create fine gradients and shadows, making it ideal for figurative drawing and highlighting intricate details in artworks..
